ABOUT US

Why “Turtle Bread”?


Because great bread takes time.

Since November of 1994, we’ve been taking our time to hand-craft flavorful artisan breads, pastries, soups, cookies and pies. We’ve branched out along the way – expanding to include a wood fire pizza restaurant, a French bistro, and a rustic Italian café – all the while maintaining our dedication to fresh, flavorful food served in a welcoming neighborhood environment. We love that we’re the kind of place our customers visit to enjoy a weekend breakfast with their families, to pick up some cheese and fresh bread after work to serve to dinner guests, to feast on a romantic candlelit anniversary dinner, or to simply treat themselves to a piece of delicious pie made from scratch.

We hope you’ll visit us soon!

Who cut the cheese?


No need for embarrassment – it was us. All of our locations stock a hearty selection of cheeses and other deli delights ready to slice and arrange on a tray for your next party, or cut into individual serving sizes that pair perfectly with a slice of our baguette for a quick lunch. 		Breakfast, Lunch, or Dinner?

Early birds can stop by any of our three locations for a full hot breakfast menu or for a quick bite before work. If lunch is on your mind, you have a world of options, from full menu meals to a simple delicious soup and sandwich. For dinner Pizza Biga offers full dinner service at our 48th and Chicago location. 		Soup's Fantastic

Every day our soup chef, Daniel, prepares fresh soups for each of Turtle Bread’s three locations. While Tomato Basil has long been established as our number-one seller, other recipes such as Garbanzo Spinach and Chicken Tortilla are fast becoming customer favorites. Selections vary daily by location; please call your nearest Turtle Bread for a listing of the day’s soups.
